Paneer and Spinach Tikka Masala

This Indian inspired tikka masala with paneer is the perfect creamy curry and is ready in under an hour.

Ingredients

  • 40g butter
  • 2 x 200g packets paneer, cut into 3cm pieces
  • 1 brown onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 1/4 cup tikka masala curry paste
  • 1/2 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp fennel seeds
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 400g can diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup vegetable liquid stock
  • 120g baby spinach
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh coriander
  • Coriander sprigs, to serve
  • Plain Greek-style yoghurt, to serve
  • Warmed naan bread, to serve

How to make

  1. Melt half the butter in a large, deep frying pan over medium heat. Cook paneer for 1 to 2 minutes each side or until lightly browned. Transfer to a plate.
  2. Melt remaining butter in pan over medium heat. Add onion . Cook, stirring, for 5 minutes or until golden. Reduce heat to low.
  3. Add garlic , curry paste , turmeric , fennel and cinnamon . Cook, stirring, for 30 seconds or until fragrant.
  4. Add tomatoes and stock . Bring to the boil. Reduce heat to low. Simmer for 15 minutes or until thickened.
  5. Stir in spinach and coriander . Cook for 3 minutes or until spinach wilts.
  6. Return paneer to pan. Cook for 1 to 2 minutes or until heated through. Top with extra coriander . Serve with yoghurt and warm naan.
